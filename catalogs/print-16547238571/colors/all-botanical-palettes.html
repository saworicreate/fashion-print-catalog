Usage Guidelines

  • Print Applications: All colors are optimized for CMYK conversion and maintain vibrancy in print
  • Digital Design: RGB values ensure consistent display across devices and screens
  • Accessibility: Each palette includes sufficient contrast ratios for text and background combinations
  • Flexibility: Colors can be used individually or in various combinations within each theme
  • Scalability: Palettes work effectively from small icons to large format designs
  • Brand Integration: Each theme maintains visual cohesion while allowing for brand customization
