# NEO POP MODERNISM
## 2100 Spring/Summer Collection

A fusion of bold pop art aesthetics with modernist sophistication, reimagined for the 22nd century. This collection explores the intersection of digital culture, sustainable futures, and timeless design principles.

**Color Philosophy:** Vibrant neon hues balanced with soft pastels and clean neutrals, reflecting both optimistic futurism and organic harmony.

## Production Guidelines

- **File Format:** High-resolution seamless tiles, minimum 4096x4096px
- **Color Mode:** RGB for digital, convertible to CMYK for print production
- **Repeat Testing:** All patterns must tile perfectly in all directions with no visible seams
- **Scale Variations:** Each pattern should be tested at multiple scales for different applications
- **Sustainability:** Color palette optimized for eco-friendly reactive dyes and digital printing technologies available in 2100
